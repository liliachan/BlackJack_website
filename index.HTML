The Introduction

We are a team of four students at the Olin College of Engineering taking a course called Principles of Engineering where we create an integrated project with nontrivial mechanical, electrical, and software components.

Blackjack anyone?

Project BlackJack was designed with Vegas in mind. Our goals while creating it were for it to perform all of the tasks a dealer at a Black Jack table would: dealing individual cards to all players around a table, and dealing more to them when asked. It is broken up into 4 electro-mechanical subsystems.
